Setting Up Bait Fishing

In order to set up bait fishing you will need a couple of things.

    All of the following items should probably not be rated above ten pounds. Beginners should start small, and as such catch smaller fish. You will probably find pre-built rods in the store, but the list will feature everything just in case.
  • A Spinning Rod and Reel
  • A line for the reel
  • Hooks and Baits. Worms and small minnows are my personal favorites.
